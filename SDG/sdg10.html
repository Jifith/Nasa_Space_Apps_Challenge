Goal 10: Reduced Inequality

  • Home
  • Other SDGs

Overview

Reduce inequality within and among countries by 2030.

Targets

  • 10.1: Achieve and sustain income growth of the bottom 40% of the population at a rate higher than the national average.
  • 10.2: Empower and promote the social, economic, and political inclusion of all.

Indicators

  • 10.1.1: Growth rates of household expenditure or income per capita among the bottom 40% of the population.
  • 10.2.1: Proportion of people living below 50% of median income, by age, sex, and persons with disabilities.
