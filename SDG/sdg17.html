Goal 17: Partnerships for the Goals

  • Home
  • Other SDGs

Overview

Strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development by 2030.

Targets

  • 17.1: Strengthen domestic resource mobilization to improve domestic capacity for tax and other revenue collection.
  • 17.2: Promote the development, transfer, and diffusion of environmentally sound technologies.

Indicators

  • 17.1.1: Total government revenue as a proportion of GDP.
  • 17.2.1: Proportion of individuals using the Internet.
