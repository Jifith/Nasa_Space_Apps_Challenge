Goal 2: Zero Hunger

  • Home
  • Other SDGs

Overview

End hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture by 2030.

Targets

  • 2.1: End hunger and ensure access to safe, nutritious, and sufficient food year-round.
  • 2.2: End all forms of malnutrition by 2025.

Indicators

  • 2.1.1: Prevalence of undernourishment.
  • 2.2.1: Prevalence of stunting among children under 5 years of age.
