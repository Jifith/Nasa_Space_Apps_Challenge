Goal 5: Gender Equality

  • Home
  • Other SDGs

Overview

Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls by 2030.

Targets

  • 5.1: End all forms of discrimination against all women and girls everywhere.
  • 5.2: Eliminate all forms of violence against all women and girls.

Indicators

  • 5.1.1: Whether or not legal frameworks are in place to promote gender equality.
  • 5.2.1: Proportion of women and girls subjected to violence.
