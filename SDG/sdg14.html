Goal 14: Life Below Water

  • Home
  • Other SDGs

Overview

Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources for sustainable development by 2030.

Targets

  • 14.1: Prevent and significantly reduce marine pollution of all kinds.
  • 14.2: Sustainably manage and protect marine and coastal ecosystems.

Indicators

  • 14.1.1: Index of coastal eutrophication and floating plastic debris density.
  • 14.2.1: Proportion of national exclusive economic zones managed using ecosystem-based approaches.
