Goal 15: Life on Land

  • Home
  • Other SDGs

Overview

Protect, restore, and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems by 2030.

Targets

  • 15.1: Ensure the conservation, restoration, and sustainable use of terrestrial and inland freshwater ecosystems.
  • 15.2: Promote the implementation of sustainable management of forests.

Indicators

  • 15.1.1: Forest area as a proportion of total land area.
  • 15.2.1: Progress towards sustainable forest management.
