Goal 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

Overview

Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns by 2030.

Targets

  • 12.1: Implement the 10-Year Framework of Programmes on Sustainable Consumption and Production.
  • 12.3: Halve per capita global food waste at the retail and consumer levels.

Indicators

  • 12.1.1: Number of countries with SCP national action plans or SCP mainstreamed as a priority in national policies.
  • 12.3.1: Global food loss index.
