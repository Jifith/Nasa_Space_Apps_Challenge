Goal 13: Climate Action

  • Home
  • Other SDGs

Overview

Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts by 2030.

Targets

  • 13.1: Strengthen resilience and adaptive capacity to climate-related hazards and natural disasters.
  • 13.2: Integrate climate change measures into national policies, strategies, and planning.

Indicators

  • 13.1.1: Number of countries with national and local disaster risk reduction strategies.
  • 13.2.1: Number of countries that have communicated the establishment or operationalization of an integrated policy/strategy/plan.
